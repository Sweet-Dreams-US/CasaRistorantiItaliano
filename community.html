N° 01 · Programs

How we show up.

I.

Fundraising Dinners

We host private dinners where 25% of every check (food + non-alcoholic beverage) goes directly to your school, parish, or nonprofit. Pick a Tuesday or Wednesday, send the invite, we set the table. Minimum 30 guests; we've done up to 220.

II.

Spirit Nights

Pick a weeknight, your supporters dine at any Casa, mention your group at the door. We donate 20% of every check from your supporters back to your group. No private booking required.

III.

Silent Auction Donations

Casa gift cards (typically $50-$200) for school auctions, charity galas, and church festivals. Submit a request 4-6 weeks ahead — we ship directly to your event.

IV.

First Responders

A standing 15% off any meal, any Casa, any day, for FWPD, FWFD, and Allen County EMS in uniform. No coupon, no questions.

V.

Teacher Tuesdays

Every Tuesday, FWCS, NACS, EACS, and SACS teachers eat with 20% off — show your school ID. Because all of us learned from somebody.

VI.

Casa Cares Meal Drives

Two times a year — Thanksgiving and back-to-school — we cook and donate 1,000+ family-style meals to Community Harvest Food Bank and local shelters.
