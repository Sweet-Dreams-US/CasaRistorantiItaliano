N° 03 · How It Works

From Casa, to your door.

  1. Tell us the date and headcount. Use the form below or call any of the four locations directly.
  2. We send a quote within one business day. Pick a curated menu or build your own.
  3. A 25% deposit holds the date. Final headcount is due 72 hours out.
  4. We deliver hot, set up, and (if staffed) stay through the meal. All chafers, serving utensils, and pans included.

24-hour minimum on Family Carryout Pans, 48 hours preferred. Larger orders — call ahead a week if you can.
