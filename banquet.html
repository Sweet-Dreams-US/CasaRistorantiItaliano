Banquet Room · La Sala

A long table, una sala for you.

Each Casa keeps a private banquet room for the moments worth a long table. Rehearsal dinners, communions, retirement parties, work celebrations, the occasional second wedding. We host the whole night — bread first, espresso last.

A typical evening, set up at the Stellhorn banquet room for a 24-top.

N° 01 · Le Sale

Four rooms, four shapes, four addresses.

01.

Casa Grille · Dupont

Capacity: up to 50 seated · 70 standing
  • Private street entrance
  • Built-in AV with podium mic & HDMI input
  • Adjacent bar with optional dedicated bartender
  • Best for: rehearsal dinners, retirements
02.

Casa Grille Italiano · Stellhorn

Capacity: up to 60 seated · 90 standing
  • Largest of the four — long single table for 30+
  • Wood-fired oven visible from the room
  • Patio attaches in season for cocktail extension
  • Best for: weddings, milestone birthdays
03.

Casa! Ristorante · Jefferson

Capacity: up to 80 seated · 120 standing
  • Two adjoining private rooms (combine or split)
  • Open kitchen view from the larger PDR
  • Full AV, projector, dance floor option
  • Best for: receptions, communions, large groups
04.

Casa Ristorante Italiano · Parnell

Capacity: up to 36 seated · intimate
  • Smallest, candlelit, most intimate
  • Closest to downtown — 5 min from courthouse
  • Can buy out the entire dining room for 60+
  • Best for: anniversaries, business dinners
