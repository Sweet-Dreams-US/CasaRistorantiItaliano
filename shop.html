N° 01 · The Original

The Casaburo dressing, bottled.

For forty-eight years our peppery house dressing was something you could only get at the table. Around 2008, neighbors started asking to buy a jar to take home — so we put it in one. Same five ingredients, same proportions, same recipe Tom wrote on the back of a guest check in 1977.

Lasts six weeks in your fridge once opened. Goes on every salad. Tastes especially good on a sandwich.
