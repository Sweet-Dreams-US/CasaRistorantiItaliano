N° 01 · Chef's Monthly Feature

Ossobuco di Vitello.

This month from the kitchen: a Milanese classic. Bone-in veal shank, three-hour braise in white wine and soffritto, finished with a bright gremolata of parsley, lemon zest and garlic. We serve it the only proper way — over saffron risotto Milanese.

Available all of May at all four locations, dinner only. Limited each night, sells through fast on weekends.

Reserve a Table → $36 · paired wine $14
